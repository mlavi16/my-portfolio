Bucket List

A bucket list is a number of experiences or achievements that a person hopes to have or accomplish during their lifetime.
This is my bucket list.

What do I want to do today? Click the button to find out! -------

1

Go Skydiving

Jumping out of a plane, free falling, then slowing down with a parachute is the thrill of a lifetime.

2

Go to Machu Picchu

Visit Machu Picchulocated in the Cusco Region of Peru. Machu Picchu stands 2,430 m above sea-level, in the middle of a tropical mountain forest, in an extraordinarily beautiful setting. It was probably the most amazing urban creation of the Inca Empire at its height; its giant walls, terraces and ramps seem as if they have been cut naturally in the continuous rock escarpments. The natural setting, on the eastern slopes of the Andes, encompasses the upper Amazon basin with its rich diversity of flora and fauna.

3

Go to the Grand Canyon

Carved by the Colorado River, the Grand Canyon is over a mile deep, and 277 miles long. In order to really experience the Grand Canyon, drive to the North Rim, 45 miles south of Jacob Lake. Then, hike the ten miles to the South Rim near Tusayan. This is called the Rim to Rim hike.

The current temperature at the grand canyon is: Celcius

4

See the Great Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza pyramid complex bordering what is now El Giza, Egypt. It is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, and the only one to remain largely intact.

5

Fly in a hot air balloon

You’ll be hard pressed to find a better view. Flying up to 5,000 feet above the ground, hot air balloons offer an incredible vantage point to appreciate just how beautiful the land below is.

6

Hike the entire Appalachian trail

Why would I want to hike the Appalachian Trail? 1.You learn to embrace being challenged in unpredictable ways. 2.You get an opportunity to create a better version of yourself. 3.Because of the encounters with wonderful, eccentric people. 4.You’ll be a part of a great traveling community. 5.You get to experience living the simple life. 6.Hiking the Appalachian Trail will help your resume stand out post-adventure. 7.It adds another checkmark to your bucket list! :)

7

Learn Archery

Archery is a great sport for building confidence quickly. The feeling of shooting a bow and accomplishing your goal – whether it’s drilling a bull’s eye or executing a great shot – helps you build self-esteem and enjoy a sense of accomplishment.
